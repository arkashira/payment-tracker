# Product Requirements Document: Payment Tracker
## Overview
The Payment Tracker is a payment processing and transaction monitoring platform designed to provide real-time status updates and automated issue resolution for financial service companies and payment processing firms. The goal of this product is to increase the efficiency and reliability of payment processing, reducing errors and improving customer satisfaction.

## Problem Statement
Financial service companies and payment processing firms face numerous challenges in payment processing, including delayed or lost transactions, incorrect settlements, and inadequate customer support. These issues can lead to significant financial losses, damage to reputation, and decreased customer trust.

## Target Users
The primary target users of the Payment Tracker are:

* Financial service companies
* Payment processing firms
* Merchants and businesses that rely on payment processing services

## Goals
The Payment Tracker aims to achieve the following goals:

* Provide real-time status updates for payment transactions
* Automate issue resolution for payment processing errors
* Improve customer satisfaction through efficient and reliable payment processing
* Reduce financial losses due to payment processing errors

## Key Features
The following key features are prioritized for the initial release:

1. **Real-time Transaction Monitoring**: Provide real-time updates on payment transaction status, including processing, settlement, and completion.
2. **Automated Issue Resolution**: Implement automated workflows to resolve payment processing errors, such as transaction retries, error notifications, and settlement corrections.
3. **Transaction Analytics**: Offer detailed analytics and insights on payment transaction trends, errors, and performance metrics.
4. **Customizable Alerts and Notifications**: Allow users to configure alerts and notifications for specific payment transaction events, such as transaction failures or settlements.
5. **Integration with Payment Gateways**: Support integration with popular payment gateways, such as Stripe, PayPal, and Authorize.net.
6. **User Management and Access Control**: Implement role-based access control and user management to ensure secure access to payment transaction data.

## Success Metrics
The success of the Payment Tracker will be measured by the following metrics:

* **Transaction Success Rate**: The percentage of successful payment transactions processed through the platform.
* **Error Resolution Rate**: The percentage of payment processing errors resolved through automated workflows.
* **Customer Satisfaction**: Measured through surveys, feedback forms, and support ticket resolution rates.
* **Revenue Growth**: The increase in revenue generated by financial service companies and payment processing firms using the Payment Tracker.

## Scope
The initial release of the Payment Tracker will focus on the key features listed above. The following items are out of scope for the initial release:

* **Support for Cryptocurrency Payments**: Support for cryptocurrency payments will be considered for future releases.
* **Machine Learning-based Fraud Detection**: Integration with machine learning-based fraud detection systems will be considered for future releases.
* **Support for Non-Standard Payment Protocols**: Support for non-standard payment protocols will be considered for future releases.

## Out-of-Scope
The following items are explicitly out of scope for the Payment Tracker:

* **Core Payment Processing**: The Payment Tracker will not provide core payment processing services, such as payment gateway services or merchant account management.
* **Compliance and Regulatory Services**: The Payment Tracker will not provide compliance and regulatory services, such as anti-money laundering (AML) or know-your-customer (KYC) checks.
